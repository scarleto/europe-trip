🏘️ Jordaan

A former working-class quarter of narrow canals, courtyard gardens and independent streets.

AREA

Jordaan, west of the canal ring

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Westermarkt or Marnixstraat

ALLOW

2–3 hours

Visit details

Opening hours

Public neighbourhood streets are always open; shops and markets keep individual hours.

How to get there

Take tram 13 or 17 to Westermarkt/Marnixstraat, or walk west from Centraal through the canal ring.

Open in Google Maps

What to know

  • Walk Brouwersgracht, Noordermarkt and the quieter streets between Westerstraat and Elandsgracht.
  • Look for hidden hofjes, small galleries and the shift from grand canal houses to intimate lanes.
  • Combine it naturally with the Anne Frank House and western canal ring.

Plan your visit

  • Go early before shopping streets become busy.
  • Remember that most buildings are homes.
  • Market days add atmosphere but also crowds.

Food & facilities nearby

Food nearby

Westerstraat, Noordermarkt and the side streets around Elandsgracht have many cafés and bakeries.

Toilets nearby

Public provision is limited; use customer toilets in cafés or facilities near Westerkerk and major markets.

Photography & safety

Photography tips

Photograph quiet side canals early. Look for hofjes, leaning houses, door details and reflections rather than only wide views.

Safety tips

Respect residential doorways and courtyards, stay out of cycle lanes and secure belongings at busy market times.
