🚋 Amsterdam Local Transport

Trams do most of the useful central-city work, with metro, buses and free IJ ferries filling the gaps.

AREA

GVB network across Amsterdam

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Tap in and out with the same payment method

ALLOW

Most central journeys take 10–25 minutes

What to know

  • Contactless bank cards and devices can be used with OVpay. Every traveller needs their own card or device.
  • Always check in when boarding or entering and check out at the end, using exactly the same card or device.
  • Amsterdam Centraal is the main interchange for trams, metro and ferries.

Plan your visit

  • Walking is often quickest within the canal ring.
  • Do not stand in cycle lanes while waiting or checking a map.
  • Use the GVB app for live diversions and choose a day ticket only after comparing likely journeys.
