🎒 Pickpocket Awareness

Busy transport and landmark crowds require calm, ordinary precautions.

Carry

Keep phones and wallets in closed front pockets or a zipped cross-body bag. Do not hang valuables on chair backs or leave a phone on a café table.

Crowds

Be most alert at ticket machines, train doors, escalators, photo viewpoints and when someone creates a distraction or presses unusually close.

If something goes

Move somewhere safe, freeze affected cards, use device tracking without confronting anyone, and report the loss if documentation is needed.
