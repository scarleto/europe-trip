🚇 Public Transport Safety

Simple habits for trams, buses, metros and stations.

Boarding

Let passengers off, keep bags closed and avoid standing at the door with a phone exposed. Move away from anyone behaving aggressively.

Tickets

Use official machines, apps and counters. Ignore strangers offering to sell or help with tickets, and retain proof of travel until outside the system.

At night

Use well-lit stops, check the final service, stay together and move carriages or seek staff if a situation feels wrong.

Verify before relying on this: official guidance .
