☎️ Emergency Contacts

One number works for immediate help in all four countries.

Call 112

Dial 112 free from a fixed or mobile phone for police, ambulance or fire services anywhere in the European Union.

Say

Give the city, exact location or landmark, what happened, how many people need help and a callback number. Ask for English if needed.

Not urgent

For non-emergency assistance use local police, a pharmacy, medical provider or the appropriate official service rather than 112.

Verify before relying on this: official guidance .
