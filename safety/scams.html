🚫 Common Scams

Recognise the approach, decline clearly and keep walking.

Street approaches

Ignore petitions, friendship bracelets, found-ring stories, shell games and unsolicited help at machines. These often create distraction or pressure.

Payments

Confirm prices before ordering, inspect the card terminal amount, choose local currency and never let a payment card disappear from sight.

Tickets

Buy attraction and transport tickets from official sites, apps, counters or machines—not from people approaching queues.

Verify before relying on this: official guidance .
