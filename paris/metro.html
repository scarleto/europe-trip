🚇 Using the Paris Métro

The Métro is the quickest way to cross Paris once you learn to follow line numbers and their terminal directions.

AREA

16 numbered lines across Paris

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Look for a red Métro sign

ALLOW

Most central trips take 15–30 minutes

What to know

  • Choose the line number, then identify the final station in your direction of travel. Platform signs use that terminus rather than compass directions.
  • At an interchange, follow the coloured line number and terminus signs until the next platform.
  • Keep your validated ticket or pass until you have completely left the station.

Plan your visit

  • The metro map PDF above is available offline once cached.
  • Let passengers leave before boarding and move away from the doors.
  • Some stations involve long corridors and stairs; use the official journey planner when step-free access matters.
