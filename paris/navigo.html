🎫 Tickets & Navigo Easy

A simple way to pay for local Métro, train, RER, bus and tram journeys without keeping paper tickets.

AREA

Métro and RER stations

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Buy at a staffed counter or ticket machine

ALLOW

Allow 5–10 minutes to set up

What to know

  • Navigo Easy is a reusable, anonymous card. Each traveller needs their own card when travelling together.
  • Load the ticket that matches the journey, then tap the card on the reader at every gate or validator.
  • Keep the card separate from contactless bank cards to avoid tapping the wrong one.

Plan your journey

  • For ordinary journeys within the city, ask for the current Métro–Train–RER ticket.
  • Check the remaining balance at a machine or in the Île-de-France Mobilités app.
  • Keep each traveller's card separate so everyone can validate individually.
