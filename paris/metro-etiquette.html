🤝 Paris Métro Etiquette

A few small habits make crowded Métro journeys calmer for everyone.

AREA

All Métro and RER services

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Stand right on escalators

ALLOW

Quick reference

What to know

  • Wait to the side of the doors and let everyone off before boarding.
  • Fold pushchairs when practical, remove backpacks in crowded carriages and keep bags out of the aisle.
  • Offer priority seats to older, pregnant or disabled passengers and keep calls and music quiet.

Plan your visit

  • Have your ticket or pass ready before reaching the gate.
  • Move down inside the carriage instead of stopping at the doors.
  • A simple bonjour, pardon or merci goes a long way.
