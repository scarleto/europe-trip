City guides / Paris / La Galerie Dior

🧵 La Galerie Dior

An immersive fashion exhibition in the historic Avenue Montaigne building associated with Christian Dior's couture house.

Booked: 2 July 2026 at 12:30 · Four visitors
AREA

30 Avenue Montaigne, 8th arrondissement

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Alma–Marceau or Franklin D. Roosevelt

ALLOW

90 minutes–2 hours

Visit details

Opening hours

Generally Wednesday–Monday 11:00–19:00; closed Tuesday. Timed entry and exceptional closures apply.

How to get there

Take Métro line 9 to Alma–Marceau or line 1/9 to Franklin D. Roosevelt, then walk along Avenue Montaigne.

Open in Google Maps

What to know

  • The displays connect silhouettes, sketches, photographs and atelier craft across the house's history.
  • The colour-gradient installation and miniature dresses reward slow looking rather than a rushed photo stop.
  • The building's relationship to the original couture address gives the exhibition unusual context.

Plan your visit

  • Timed tickets are the safest option; use the official gallery website.
  • Large bags and food are restricted, so travel light.
  • Photography rules can vary by display; follow gallery signage and avoid blocking narrow rooms.

Food & facilities nearby

Food nearby

The gallery café is inside; Avenue Montaigne and Rue de Marignan have cafés, with broader choice toward the Champs-Élysées.

Toilets nearby

Visitor toilets are inside La Galerie Dior after entry.

Photography & safety

Photography tips

The colour installations reward centred compositions. Photography is normally personal-use only; follow room signage.

Safety tips

Use the official timed-ticket site, travel light for security and avoid blocking narrow exhibition rooms.
