🚋 Prague Local Transport

Trams are the most scenic way around central Prague, while three Metro lines handle longer cross-city journeys.

AREA

PID network within Prague

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Tickets are time-based across tram, bus and metro

ALLOW

Most central trips take 10–30 minutes

What to know

  • Buy the right duration before travel and validate a paper ticket once at the start; app tickets must be activated before boarding.
  • Transfers are included while the ticket remains valid, so do not validate the same paper ticket again.
  • On escalators stand right, and on trams watch the stop display before moving toward the doors.

Plan your visit

  • The PID Lítačka app combines route planning and mobile tickets.
  • Central Prague is very walkable, but cobbles and hills make trams useful.
  • Ticket inspections are common and plain-clothes inspectors show identification.
