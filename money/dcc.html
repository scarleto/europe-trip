⚠️ Avoid Dynamic Currency Conversion

When a terminal or ATM offers pounds, choose the local currency instead.

What DCC is

Dynamic Currency Conversion lets the merchant or ATM convert the amount into pounds using its own displayed exchange rate and any markup.

What to press

In France, the Netherlands and Germany choose EUR. In Czechia choose CZK. Decline conversion, guaranteed rate, GBP or home currency.

If pressured

Ask for the transaction to be cancelled and rerun in local currency. Check the amount and currency before entering a PIN or tapping approve.

Verify before relying on this: official guidance .
