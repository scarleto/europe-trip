💶 Cash Requirements

Cards are widely accepted, but a modest cash reserve remains useful.

Euros

Paris, Amsterdam and Berlin use euros. Keep a small mix of notes and coins for markets, small cafés, lockers and unexpected card failures.

Czech koruna

Prague uses Czech koruna (CZK), not the euro. Some tourist businesses accept euros at a poor rate; paying in koruna is normally clearer.

How much

Avoid carrying the whole trip budget. Take enough for one low-spend day, distribute it securely, and replenish from a bank-operated ATM if necessary.
