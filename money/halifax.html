💳 Halifax Clarity Card

Using the travel credit card carefully abroad.

Paying

Pay in the local currency. Halifax currently advertises no foreign transaction fee for Clarity card purchases, but check the terms attached to the actual account before travelling.

Cash withdrawals

Halifax advertises no cash-withdrawal fee for Clarity, but cash interest runs from the transaction date and the ATM owner may add a separate fee. A debit card may be cheaper for cash.

Good habits

Carry a second payment method, keep notifications enabled, and repay the statement in full. Never approve a transaction amount you do not recognise.
