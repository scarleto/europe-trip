🪙 Currencies

The two currencies used across these four city guides.

EUR — euro

Used in Paris, Amsterdam and Berlin. Notes are shared across the euro area; coin designs vary but remain valid throughout it.

CZK — Czech koruna

Used in Prague. Prices may be shown as Kč. The Czech Republic does not use the euro as its official currency.

Conversion

Use a trusted card or live reference rate for orientation. Exchange rates move, so the guide deliberately does not store a fixed rate.
