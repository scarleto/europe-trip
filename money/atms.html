🏧 Using ATMs

Choose reputable machines and read every conversion screen.

Choose the machine

Prefer an ATM physically attached to a recognised bank. Avoid freestanding machines with unclear branding or unusually high fees.

Before confirming

Check the withdrawal amount, the local operator fee and the currency. Cancel if the screen is confusing or the fee is unacceptable.

Stay secure

Shield the PIN, put cash and card away before leaving, and use another machine if anything looks altered or someone offers unsolicited help.
