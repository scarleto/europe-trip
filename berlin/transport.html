🚇 Berlin Local Transport

One integrated ticket covers U-Bahn, S-Bahn, trams, buses and ferries within the chosen fare zones.

AREA

BVG and S-Bahn network

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Most central sights are in zones AB

ALLOW

Most city journeys take 15–40 minutes

What to know

  • U-Bahn lines are marked U; S-Bahn lines are marked S. Regional trains can also be useful within the valid zones.
  • Paper tickets usually need validation before travel; digital tickets need activation in the app before boarding.
  • A single ticket permits changes in one direction during its validity, but not a return journey.

Plan your visit

  • Use the BVG app for live routing and platform information.
  • For several journeys, compare the current 24-hour individual and small-group tickets.
  • There are no ticket gates: inspections happen on vehicles and platforms.
