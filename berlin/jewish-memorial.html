◼ Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe

Peter Eisenman's field of concrete stelae creates a disorienting space of remembrance near Brandenburg Gate.

AREA

Cora-Berliner-Straße 1

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Brandenburger Tor

ALLOW

45–90 minutes

What to know

  • Walk quietly through the field as the ground falls and the stelae rise around you.
  • The underground Information Centre gives names, family histories and European context to the abstract memorial above.
  • The site commemorates six million Jewish victims of the Holocaust and should be treated accordingly.

Plan your visit

  • The field is open continuously; the Information Centre has separate hours and security.
  • Do not climb, jump or pose playfully on the stelae.
  • Allow emotional breathing room before moving to the next sight.
