🎨 East Side Gallery

More than a kilometre of the former Berlin Wall became an open-air gallery after reunification.

AREA

Mühlenstraße, Friedrichshain

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Warschauer Straße or Ostbahnhof

ALLOW

60–90 minutes

Visit details

Opening hours

The outdoor gallery is accessible at all times and free to visit.

How to get there

Take S/U-Bahn to Warschauer Straße or S-Bahn to Ostbahnhof and walk along Mühlenstraße.

Open in Google Maps

What to know

  • Walk the full length rather than stopping only at the most reproduced murals.
  • The works reflect the optimism and uncertainty of 1990, while the surviving concrete keeps the history physically present.
  • Cross toward the Spree at Oberbaumbrücke for a contrasting city and river view.

Plan your visit

  • The pavement can be narrow and busy; keep cycle space clear.
  • Morning offers cleaner light and fewer groups.
  • Do not add marks or touch painted surfaces.

Food & facilities nearby

Food nearby

Warschauer Straße, Simon-Dach-Straße and the Mercedes Platz area have plentiful cafés.

Toilets nearby

Public toilets are available around Ostbahnhof and major commercial venues near the eastern end.

Photography & safety

Photography tips

Morning light falls more evenly on many murals. Step back carefully and keep the cycle path clear.

Safety tips

The pavement is narrow and busy. Do not touch or mark the murals, and watch traffic when crossing for wider views.
