🏛️ Reichstag Building

Germany's parliament combines a turbulent historic shell with Norman Foster's transparent glass dome.

AREA

Platz der Republik

NEAREST TRANSPORT

Bundestag or Brandenburger Tor

ALLOW

60–90 minutes

Visit details

Opening hours

The dome is generally open daily 08:00–24:00, with last admission around 21:45. Advance registration and ID are required.

How to get there

Take U5 to Bundestag or S/U-Bahn to Brandenburger Tor, then walk across Platz der Republik.

Open in Google Maps

What to know

  • The spiral ramps reveal the city while the mirrored cone brings daylight into the parliamentary chamber below.
  • From the roof, identify the government quarter, Brandenburg Gate and the line of the Tiergarten.
  • The surviving nineteenth-century stonework and modern interventions deliberately expose different layers of German history.

Plan your visit

  • Dome visits are free but advance registration and identification are required.
  • Use only the official Bundestag registration site.
  • Arrive before the stated entry time for security screening.

Food & facilities nearby

Food nearby

The rooftop restaurant requires separate advance booking; cafés are available around Hauptbahnhof and Unter den Linden.

Toilets nearby

Accessible toilets are available inside after security.

Photography & safety

Photography tips

The spiral ramps create strong geometric images; use the roof for city views and avoid photographing security procedures.

Safety tips

Bring the same ID used to register, arrive early for screening and use only the official Bundestag booking system.
